Bind your settings and / or Parameterizable objects to a syntax of your choice. For Parameterizables two syntaxes are supported: a specialized syntax and JSON. You can also develop your own syntax:
  • extend the provided abstract classes for parsing and serialization
  • extend the AbstractBindingHelperDecorator
  • your BindingHelper must be Threadsafe and provide a no argument constructor
  • Your parser should provide a constructor accepting a Reader as argument
  • Your serializer should provide a no argument constructor
  • use the provided factory implementations in your applications to get hold of a parser, serializer or bindinghelper
